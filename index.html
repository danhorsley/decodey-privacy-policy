Decodey does not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal data. We don't have servers, we don't track you, and we can't see your information.

What We DON'T Do

  • We don't collect any personal information
  • We don't track your activity
  • We don't have access to your data
  • We don't run any servers or databases
  • We don't use analytics or advertising

Local Storage Only

All game data stays on YOUR device:

  • Game progress and statistics
  • Settings and preferences
  • Daily challenge completion
  • Tutorial status

This data never leaves your device and we have no way to access it.

Apple Game Center Integration

If you choose to sign in to Game Center:
  • Your game scores are sent directly to Apple's Game Center service
  • Your Game Center nickname may appear on public leaderboards
  • This is completely optional - you can play without Game Center
  • This data goes directly to Apple, not to us
  • We never receive or have access to your Game Center information

Game Center is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy

Sign in with Apple (Optional)

If you use Sign in with Apple:

  • Authentication is handled entirely by Apple
  • We only receive a unique identifier to save your progress
  • Your email and personal details are never shared with us
  • This is optional - you can play as a guest

Children's Privacy

Decodey is safe for all ages. Since we don't collect or have access to any personal data, children's privacy is inherently protected.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Deleting the app removes all game data
  • You can reset progress in the app settings
  • Game Center scores can be managed through your Apple ID settings
